About

Product designer
by training,
brand strategist
by practice.

I started building things — objects, spaces, systems — and somewhere along the way realized the most interesting design problems aren't visual. They're narrative. How does a brand make someone feel something? How do you translate a complex idea into something a person actually wants to engage with?

That's the thread connecting everything I do: financial education, consumer brands, content systems, experimental visual work. Design as a tool for communication, not just aesthetics.

Open to freelance projects and full-time creative direction roles.
